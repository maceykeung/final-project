ABOUT ME

Macey Keung

Macey Keung is an artist and filmmaker, as well as a natural entrepreneur and social butterfly. Whether it is drawing, painting, graphic design, photography, fashion, crafting, or making films, she has been a creator ever since she can remember. Growing up between New York, Shanghai, and Berkeley, she is now a Media major and Studio Art minor at UC San Diego as part of the Chancellor’s Scholars program. At only age 18, she founded her own zine publication and media platform, Antifragile Zine, to give other marginalized youth like herself a safe space to create together, speak their truth, and develop their creative aspirations. This is what made her realize that – youth, art, and community are the catalysts for radical change. The mission of making others feel seen in art, connecting and platforming the work of other young artists, brings her meaning beyond just herself as an artist. This is ultimately what drives her forward, as Macey is a firm believer that the most powerful thing about art is its unequivocal ability to connect people and inspire change.
